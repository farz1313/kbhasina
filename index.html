About the School

Established in 1989, Khaja Bandanawaz Hasina Urdu Girls High School is an all-girls institution located in an urban area of Ausa. The school is led by Headmistress Shabana Fatima Ahsanulla Quadri and is committed to modern, inclusive education for girls.
